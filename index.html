Lions

Lions are found throughout Africa, are endangered and vulnerable. These animals live in groups of as many as 30 lions, and this is is referred to as a pride. They work together as a team to ensure the survival of the pride. The average life-span of a lion is 10-14 years, and they can run up to 50 miles per hour.



Male lions can be distinguished from their female counter-parts through their manes. The females are the ones often hunting and providing for the pride. A lion can eat over 60 pounds of food in one meal!
